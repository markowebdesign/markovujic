Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

If you still have questions or are ready to start a project, feel free to contact me directly!

What is the difference between a WordPress site and a custom solution?

WordPress is for fast, standard websites and e-commerce. Custom solutions (HTML/CSS/JS) are for unique, complex functions and top performance.

How long does it take to build a website and how is the price determined?

Production usually takes 2 to 8 weeks. The price is formed individually, based on the scope of work, number of pages and required functionalities.

Do you do only design or also coding (development)?

I provide a complete service: from the creation of visual identity and design, to the implementation and coding of a functional, optimized site.

What is required from the client before we start cooperation?

We need basic information about your business, goals, as well as the materials you have (eg logo, text, photos).
