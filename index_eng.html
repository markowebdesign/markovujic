Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

If you still have questions or are ready to start a project, feel free to contact me directly!

What is the difference between a WordPress site and a custom solution?

WordPress is for fast, standard websites and e-commerce. Custom solutions (HTML/CSS/JS) are for unique, complex functionalities and top performance.

How long does website development take and how is the price formed?

Development usually takes 2 to 8 weeks. The price is formed individually, based on the scope of work, number of pages, and required functionalities.

Do you only deal with design or also with coding (development)?

I provide a complete service: from creating the visual identity and design, to implementation and coding of a functional, optimized website.

What is required from the client before we start collaboration?

We need basic information about your business, goals, as well as materials you have (e.g., logo, text, photos).
